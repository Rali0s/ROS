What ROS is
A local-first desk for operators who want continuity.

ROS Beta is a desktop operating workspace for notes, references, structured research, wallets, trust posture, and a small social sidecar when the real work is done.

The point is not feature sprawl. The point is to make the daily workspace feel stable, recoverable, and readable enough to become a system you actually keep open.

Local-first

The native vault boundary, support bundle, and backup drill flow matter more than novelty.

Core desks

Vault Notes, Library, Research Vault, Wallet Vault, and Control Room are the real product center.

Sidecar features

Nostr Lounge is intentionally lighter and separate from ROS Comms. It is there to unwind, not replace the workspace.
